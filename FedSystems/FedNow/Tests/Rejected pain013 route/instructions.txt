1b. http://localhost:8770/docs (Baguette Bank MQ) -> /FIFO/out returns pain.013 file, than after 1s nothing.
2. http://localhost:8770/docs (Baguette Bank MQ) -> /send pain.014 with RJCT status
2a. http://localhost:8514/docs (FedNow Backend) -> /messages/complete message with RJCT moves from pending to complete with RJCT status
2b. http://localhost:8713/docs (Merchant MQ) -> /FIFO/out returns pain.014 with RJCT status than after 1s nothing.
